I am currently a DevOps professional in Tokyo. My other interests revolve around cloud computing, distributed systems, and big data.

Before moving to Tokyo, I was a Ph.D. student in Computer Science under the supervision of Ian Foster. I worked on scaling data-intesive scientific applications to supercomputers and other large computing infrastructures.
